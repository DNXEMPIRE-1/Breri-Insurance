Package policies

Wellness Program Package: Bundles wellness program services and incentives with group medical insurance coverage. Includes access to wellness resources, such as health assessments, coaching, fitness programs, and preventive screenings. Encourages employees to adopt healthy behaviors, manage chronic conditions, and improve overall well-being, leading to better health outcomes and reduced healthcare costs over time.

Telemedicine and Virtual Care Package: Incorporates telemedicine and virtual care services into the group medical insurance plan. Allows employees to consult with healthcare providers remotely for non-emergency medical issues through phone or video consultations. Provides convenient access to medical care, especially for minor ailments, follow-up visits, and after-hours care, reducing the need for in-person office visits and emergency room visits.

Employee Assistance Program (EAP) Package: Combines an employee assistance program with group medical insurance coverage. Offers confidential counseling, support services, and resources to employees and their families for personal and work-related issues, such as mental health, stress management, financial concerns, and substance abuse. Helps employees address personal challenges, improve well-being, and enhance productivity, leading to a healthier and more engaged workforce.

Prescription Drug Coverage Enhancement Package: Enhances prescription drug coverage options within the group medical insurance plan. Offers additional formulary tiers, specialty drug coverage, mail-order pharmacy services, and cost-saving strategies, such as generic drug substitution and medication therapy management. Helps employees access necessary medications at affordable prices, manage chronic conditions effectively, and optimize medication adherence and outcomes.
