Package policies

Home and Auto Insurance Package: Combines homeowners or renters insurance with auto insurance coverage. Provides comprehensive protection for both your home and vehicles. Coverage may include property damage, liability, theft, vandalism, personal belongings, and medical payments. Bundling these policies often qualifies you for discounts and simplifies the insurance

Health and Life Insurance Package: Bundles health insurance and life insurance coverage together. Offers financial protection for both medical expenses and future financial needs. Health insurance covers medical costs, while life insurance provides a death benefit to beneficiaries. Ensures comprehensive coverage for both healthcare needs and long-term financial security.

Travel and Accident Insurance Package: Combines travel insurance with accident insurance coverage. Offers protection for unexpected events while traveling, such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and travel-related accidents. Accident insurance provides coverage for injuries, disabilities, or fatalities resulting from accidents, whether at home or abroad. Ideal for individuals or families who travel frequently and want peace of mind while on the go.

Personal Liability and Umbrella Insurance Package: Bundles personal liability insurance with umbrella insurance coverage. Personal liability insurance protects against lawsuits and legal claims for bodily injury or property damage that you're held responsible for. Umbrella insurance provides additional liability coverage beyond the limits of your primary insurance policies, offering extra protection against large liability claims. Helps safeguard your assets and future income from costly legal judgments and lawsuits.

GROUP FUNERAL EXPENSE

Death and funerals are the last things that anyone would want to think or discuss about. However, the reality is we need to plan before it happens because they can be very expensive if not planned for. Most families face financial pressure if the person they have lost is the breadwinner. So why not put funeral costs to rest through an easy and affordable cover for your employees. Funeral Expenses (also known as last expense) Insurance reimburses the cost of funeral expenses in the event of the death of employee(s).

International   Lifetime Support   Professional   Trustworthy

The policy enables employers, welfare societies and other affinity groups to provide means of financing funeral expenses when a member passes away. The limits of cover for a group will be set according to the preference of the policy holder subject to maximum cover offered under group funeral. Once cover limits and premium payable has been agreed, these limits will be applicable to all members across board.
